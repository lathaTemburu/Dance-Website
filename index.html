Courses We Offer

We offer dedicated changing rooms, a sound system for classical music practice, and seating arrangements for parents and visitors. Our peaceful ambience helps students focus, learn, and grow with discipline and grace.

Beginner Level

Perfect for students with no prior experience. Learn the basic steps, hand gestures (mudras), and rhythm patterns essential to Kuchipudi.

Intermediate Level

For students with a basic understanding of Kuchipudi. Focuses on improving stamina, expression (abhinaya), and choreography techniques.

Advanced Level

Designed for advanced learners looking to refine their skills and perform on stage. Covers complex routines, improvisation, and solo performances.
